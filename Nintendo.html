Pokemon Sword and Shield DLC Overview

Sword and Shield was the 8th generation Pokemon game released on Nov 15, 2019.

An overview from the Sword and Shield DLC that you get 2 new locations on your map which contains some new Pokemon and older Pokemon that are set to return. In order to get this DLC you have to pay extra money on your game or else the new maps,features, and Pokemon will not show up because it would know that you didn't pay for the DLC so you would be stuck in the basic world when it's released.The 2 new main legendaries are named Urshifu in DLC part 1 and Calyrex in DLC part 2.

Your Starter could gain a Gigantamax form

Rillaboom,Cinderace and Inteleon will have access to go from Dynamax form to a Gigantamax form. The steps to getting your starter to Gigantamax is that you have to give your Pokemon max soup in order to change forms. For max Soup you must find 3 max mushrooms which could be encountered in caves and forest around the Isle of Armour . In order to farm max mushrooms you must do 3 Dynamax raids in order to gain 1 max mushroom but in order to get 3 for max soup you have to complete 9 Dynamax raids.

    New Features in DLC 1

    The new features of DLC part 1 is that you get a new location and map, you get new rivals to battle,there is a new way you can your Pokemon from Dynamax form to Gigantamax form,A new Slowpoke form,Could upgrade the Dojo at the end of the game, and have access to getting the main legendary Urshifu.

    New Features in DLC 2

    Features in DLC 2 are you get to go on side quests to find different legendary Pokemon, You get to participate in the world's champions double battle league,You will have another new Location and map,You gain another rival to battle, and complete some main side quests to get the main legendary of the second DLC Calyrex
